It was great to be part of Group 4 for the Algorithms and Programming Lab in 2026! Together, we completed 8 modules covering Python and C++ programming languages, starting from “Data Types and Variables in Python” and ending with “Functions in C++.”

For our final assignment, we created an infographic entitled *"Teen Mental Health Analysis: The Impact of Digital Habits on Adolescent Mental Health."* Using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, we analyzed data from 1,200 respondents aged 13–19 years to explore how digital habits and sleep patterns relate to stress levels among teenagers. The analysis shows that respondent demographics are relatively balanced across age groups, Instagram users with above-average stress levels slightly outnumber TikTok users, and most teenagers sleep between 5–8 hours per night. Interestingly, screen time before sleep did not show a meaningful relationship with stress levels in this dataset.
